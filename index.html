About

I'm a second-year Computer Science & Engineering student at SIMATS Engineering, Chennai, and lead of the Zyronix Tech Club, where I organize hackathons, workshops and hands-on build sessions for fellow students.

My approach is builder-first: I'd rather ship a working prototype and learn from where it breaks than stop at theory. That's taken me from an 8-bit MIPS processor on a breadboard to AI-powered civic and agriculture platforms to a quantum-classical benchmarking pipeline for power grid forecasting.

Outside coursework, I run Raghul Explains, a content brand teaching practical, logic-first coding and tech career skills.
